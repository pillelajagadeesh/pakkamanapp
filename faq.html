Frequently Asked Questions

1) How do I get Pakka app?

Pakka is available in free in Google Play Store Download here.However, you can also download it from pakka.mobi

2) Why registration is required?

All you need to do here is to provide your phone number. We need to know who you are when you login first time.

3) Why I am asked to share my location information?

We send the real time notifications to you on the go. Until and unless you share your location with us we may not be able to update you about all the excited offers, deals, or events happening in your location.

4) I do not want to disclose my contact information to unknown people.

Don't worry! We believe in protecting all the information of our users and it will not be shared with anyone. For more details, please read our privacy policy.

5) What if I want additional information about any offers?

We have a live chat window in our app. When you visit any offer page you will get a chat window at the bottom and you can use it to ask your queries to the service providers before stepping into the physical store.

6) I am a service provider. Is there a different way to register?

Service providers can also start by entering their phone number. After that you will be taken to the profile page to enter some information such as business name, store location and contact number.

7) How do I post a notification using Pakka app?

Please visit How it works (hyperlink) to find out everything about notifications. You can also fill the Registration form (hyperlink)to request for the demo.

8) Can I filter the data as per my own wish?

Yes, you can. We have a lot of categories such as food,fashion, electronics, jobs, and etc. You can choose the categories of your wish and get only those notification. Apart from that you can also add a particular store to favourites by using Add to favourite option..

9) Can I redeem an offer using Pakka app?

No, we do not have this option in our app. We provide the real time information while you are on the go. As soon as you reach your destination, our app will automatically push the nearest offers to your mobile screen. You can go to the physical store to avail an offer.
