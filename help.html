Pakka

How It Works

Pakka uses your location and category subscription details to provide the relevant and latest information near to you. It sends you a notification on your mobile screen whenever there is a new Announcement of your interest. To announce, you have to register on our app following few simple steps. Once your account is verified, you can start using our service. All the announcements will go through an approval process to make sure that the information is genuine. End users can register with their mobile number, subscribe to the categories of their choice and receive notifications from the nearby services. Please visit 'Features' page to know more about the usage and benefits of our app.

Download the app and register using your mobile number. You will receive a one-time password (OTP) to confirm you registration.Use 'Announcement' option to post any notification. When you do it very first time, you will be asked to provide additional information such as Person Name/Business Name, store location, contact number. As Pakka is open to everyone, so we have provided an option to keep your contact details public or private. It is completely up to you if you want to keep it open.When you are done with posting notification, our approval department will review your message and approve it if your message is genuine.

Based on the category you choose, your message will be pushed to all the users who have subscribed to that particular category.Enter your location using Map and you are ready to use the app. We have a lot of categories available such food, fashion, electronics, entertainment, jobs. You can choose and subscribe to the categories of your choice. You can mark a particular store as a favourite to get regular updates. Use our unique feature 'Geofencing to add or modify the circles as per your wish to get the most relevant notifications. You can also use customize option to make it more specific based on your favourite places and categories. Share the exciting offers with your friends, give your feedback by placing your valuable comments and likes. Use calendar to mark the upcoming offers and events and avoid missing out on all the excitement.
