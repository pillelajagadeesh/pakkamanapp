Features

Announcements

Register as a service provider, Announce your offerings/requirements, promote your brand following few simple steps and we will assure that your message reaches the right customers. The end users can also post notifications in various categories available.

Geofencing

Use our unique feature and mark your favourite hangout locations and we will keep you up-to-date with all the latest happenings around.

Near to me

Find out the realtime information around you in various categories like food, fashion, electronics, jobs, buy/sell/rent etc.

Get Engaged

Chat instantly with the service providers, like, comment, share, and add upcoming events information to your calendar..

Analytics

Graphical representation for service providers to see their reach and how many people have seen their posting.

Customize

Customize the app as per your favourite location and category.
