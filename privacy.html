Privacy Policy

As information such as your phone number, email address, passwords is very personal and nobody want this information to be disclosed to an unknown person. We at Pakka, are highly committed to protecting your personal information at any cost. Our privacy policy speaks about what information we collect, how we use it and what kind of sharing practices we follow.

Information we collect

Your mobile number, location, Information you provide us when you contact us for help, information you enter into our system before posting any notification such as your name/business name, physical store address.

Need of Information

Your account will be associated with your phone number and other information such as your business name, store address will be used by customers to know and locate you so that they can visit and redeem the offers in your store.

Automatically collected information

Application may collect some information automatically such as the type of mobile you use, unique device id, the IP address of your mobile, operating system, browsers.


Sharing of your information

We respect your privacy and the information you share with us is most important part of our business. We never share even a piece of your personal information with anyone. However, while creating your profile, it is up to you if you want to make your information public or private. An option is available to set your information security. Nothing will be shared without your consent.

Stop providing information

You can stop collection of your information by us anytime by uninstalling our application by following the standard procedure to uninstall. We will retain your information for as long as you are a user of our application.

Amendment

The privacy policy may change from time to time due to certain reasons. We will keep you updated on all the changes on regular basis via push notifications or SMS. However, you are advised to visit the Privacy Policy page regularly to keep yourself up to date on any amendments.

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the application, please contact us via email at info@mistminds.com
